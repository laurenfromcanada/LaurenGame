You will need to decide what kind of transportation and living accommodations you wish to purchase before leaving on your trip.

For tranportation and accommodations, you will be awarded happiness points that will give you an extra points of happiness for your first day of the trip.

Then, you will need to decide how much of your remaining money you want to budget for food and recreation.

You are not required to follow the suggested amount or use all of your savings if you do not wish.

However! You will not be able to change these amounts once you start your trip.
